FAQ
How long is standard shipping to Hawaii, Alaska, or Puerto Rico?
Shipping will take an additional 14-21 business days depending on the location. Need your gifts faster? Economy, Expedited, and Rush Shipping options are available and aligned with the normal order-by dates above.
Do your shipping timelines include weekends as shipping days?
No, shipping times are calculated in business days. Business days do not include Saturday or Sunday, and these shipping estimates do not include weekend delivery. For example, if a package ships on Friday with FedEx Rush (1 business day) it will arrive the following Monday.
Where is my order?
You can check out your order history here to find tracking for any orders placed. Sometimes tracking will not update, even if the package is in transit; usually this resolves itself within 24 hours! Please check out these production guidelines for information on how long it will take for your item to ship
Why is only 1 of my items showing tracking?
We work with multiple vendors across the US to ship your products. It is possible that if you ordered two or more separate items, they will ship in separate packages and will therefore each show up with unique tracking numbers.
Do you offer rush production?
We do not offer a rush production option at this time.
What shipping methods can I choose from?
For domestic (within the United States) deliveries, we offer four shipping options - Standard (5-8 business days), Economy (4-6 business days), Expedited (2 business days), and Rush (1 business day). These times are in addition to production time. Please keep in mind we are not responsible for shipping delays due to inclement weather, etc.

Business days do not include Saturday or Sunday, and these shipping estimates do not include weekend delivery. For example, if a package ships on Friday with FedEx Rush (1 business day) it will arrive the Monday.

Please keep in mind when ordering that these shipping times do not include the production times of our products. You can read more about production times and shipping info here. You can also find the production times each product on the product page, under the "shipping information" tab.

For international deliveries, we ship via FedEx International Economy. You can learn more about those options here. Please keep in mind we are not responsible for customs fees, shipping delays due to inclement weather, etc.
